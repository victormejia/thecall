Diagnostic Questions
Have you come to a place in your spiritual life where you know for certain that if you were to die today you would go to heaven?
These things I have written... that you may know you have eternal life. (1 John 5:13 NKJV)
Suppose you were to die today and stand before God and He were to say to you, "Why should I let you into My heaven?" What would you say?
1. Heaven is a free gift
The gift of God is eternal life. (Romans 6:32 NKJV)
2. It cannot be earned or deserved
For by grace you have been saved, through faith, and that not of yourselves, it is the gift of God, not of works, lest anyone should boast. (Ephesians 2:8-9 NKJV)
